BIM - Building Information Modeling

     “The next-generation design tool for the architecture, engineering, and construction sectors is called Building Information Modeling”

The next-generation design tool for the architecture, engineering, and construction sectors is called Building Information Modeling (BIM), and The WGM has vast experience offering collaborative BIM solutions to our international customers. has a BIM crew that is quite skilled. Building a staff with advanced BIM technical competence and familiarity with building codes, standards, and norms has been a goal of the WGM

CAD to BIM Conversion:
      BIM is a valuable resource for the WGM. Due to the continually changing business processes around the world, The WGM embraces change and assists enterprises in realising the benefits BIM may provide to their projects. According to client needs, we offer CAD to BIM conversion services.

BIM Architecture:
      Early in the process of an architectural building project, a design presentation is made, and more than half of these presentations are made to win a client pitch. Therefore, it's crucial to achieve the goal within the allotted time. This can be difficult, though, if the design concepts are still in the conceptual stage. In this case, hiring an internal employee to complete the first presentation would be equivalent to outsourcing it.

BIM Structure:
      For residential structures, commercial spaces, and industrial structures, we are experts at creating detailed and accurate structural BIM models. We have successfully finished a sizable number of structural BIM projects for residential, commercial, and industrial structures over the past ten years, ranging in complexity. No matter how complex your schematic ideas and construction drawings are, our team's enormous experience enables them to convert them into clean Revit BIM models with parametric families without a hitch.

MEPF – LOD 100 to 500:
      An industry standard known as BIM Amount of Development (LOD) measures the level of service needed by describing how the 3D geometry of the building model can be refined to various degrees. These development models were specifically created for various design phases, 3D visualisation, amounts appropriate for construction, scheduling, estimations, on-site production control, and fabrication. WGM will provide a 3D model of your projects based on the level of specificity required, using the LOD industry guidelines as a guide. In order to gain insights into how and what will be built, we also provide animation services for LOD in the sequence for virtual construction sequencing and simulation. We are skilled at providing LOD for various levels. Five LOD choices are available from 100 - 500 to WGM.
